How sessions affect attendance

  • Attendance % = (days present) ÷ (working days in session up to today)
  • Sundays are automatically excluded from working days
  • Holidays you add are also excluded from the denominator
  • Without an active session, the system falls back to old behaviour
  • Each course can have exactly one active session at a time
